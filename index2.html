Ravens Alliance Resource Calculator

This is a simple to use calculator to help our members determine the amount of resources they must acquire to update their castles.

Enter the amount of each resource you have in the proper fields. The page will calculate the total amount of food you have on hand. Enter the resources necessary for your next upgrade. The calculator will show you the quantity of resources you need to collect. A negative number indicates you have adequate resouces. Have fun!
